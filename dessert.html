1. Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Melt the chocolate and let it cool slightly.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until creamy.
  3. Mix in melted chocolate and vanilla.
  4. Whip cream until soft peaks form and fold into chocolate mixture.
  5. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form and gently fold in.
  6. Chill for 2 hours before serving.

2. Fruit Custard

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed fruits (apple, banana, grapes, etc.)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar

Steps:

  1. Mix custard powder with 1/4 cup cold milk to make a smooth paste.
  2. Boil the remaining milk and add sugar.
  3. Stir in custard paste and cook until thickened.
  4. Cool the custard completely.
  5. Add chopped fruits and mix well.
  6. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
