1. Garlic Bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C (350¬∞F).
  2. Slice the baguette and place on a baking tray.
  3. Mix garlic, butter, and olive oil in a bowl.
  4. Spread mixture on bread slices.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley and serve warm.

2. Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Toothpicks or small skewers

Steps:

  1. Thread one tomato, one basil leaf, and one mozzarella ball onto each skewer.
  2. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.
